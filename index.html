Report an Incident

If you suspect or witness any form of child abuse, report it immediately. Your report can save a life. The process is simple, safe, and can be done anonymously if you wish.

Select the type of incident, provide key details, and submit your report. Your submission will be handled confidentially, and appropriate actions will be taken by the authorities.

Report Now
How it works

Make your report in just 3 simple steps

01

Select Type of Abuse :

Choose the category that best describes the incident you are reporting. You can select from options like physical abuse, emotional abuse, sexual abuse, neglect, or bullying. If unsure, pick the category that seems closest to the incident.

02

Step 2: Provide Incident Details

Provide key details about the incident, such as when and where it happened. You can also upload photos or videos.

03

Submit Report Anonymously or With Your Contact:

You can submit the report anonymously if you prefer, or provide your contact information to receive updates or assist in the investigation. Your information will be kept confidential.

04

Step 4: Authorities Will Review and Respond

fter submitting your report, the relevant authorities will review it and take appropriate action. If needed, they may contact you for more information or updates regarding the case.

About CAIRS

Our mission is to provide a safe, secure, and anonymous platform for individuals to report cases of child abuse without fear of stigma or retaliation. We are committed to empowering victims, concerned individuals, and communities by offering a seamless way to raise alerts about incidents. Every report is directed to the appropriate authorities to ensure prompt action is taken, safeguarding children from harm.
